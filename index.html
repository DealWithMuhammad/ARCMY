Please send your requests along with relevant documents to the following links:
    referralunhcr.imaret@gmail.com
    Hotlines/WhatsApp: 6085803-017---6085814-017-----6085823-017---
    The decision to approve the request is made jointly by the Health Referral Committee, which is guided by UNHCR's Referral Health Care Guidelines. The committee consists of representatives from UNHCR and 3 NGO's, including medical and non-medical personnel, to provide comprehensive diagnostic referrals.
    Note: Currently, we are not able to support any financial transactions for cases requiring dialysis due to the breakdown of the network. Access to heart surgery, angioplasty, and cancer treatment will also depend on the availability of funds.
